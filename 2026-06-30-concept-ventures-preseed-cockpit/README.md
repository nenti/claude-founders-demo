# Pre-Seed Deal Flow Cockpit — Demo for Concept Ventures

Lean concept demo: a **pre-seed deal-flow / pipeline cockpit** tailored to a
Concept Ventures investor (Hugo Hazon, VC @ Concept Ventures, London) — the kind
of at-a-glance view that matters when you're sourcing and triaging pre-seed deals.

## Goal

Show, in one screen, what a pre-seed investor cares about:

- **KPIs** — new inbound, deals in diligence, dry powder, average first cheque.
- **Top Opportunities** — the hottest startups by fit score, with sector, round
  and current stage (UK & European pre-seed).
- **Pipeline funnel** — from inbound/screening down to term sheet, incl. conversion.

## The nyxory angle

The point of the demo for the call: this whole personalised app was **generated
and deployed live by an AI agent on nyxory** — from a single prompt to a public
URL in minutes. That's the pitch, made tangible.
